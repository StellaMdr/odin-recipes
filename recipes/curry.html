Vegetable Currz

Quick curry with 5 ingredients

This budget-friendly vegetable curry is great for students and novice cooks!

Ingredients

  • 1 tin of cannellini beans
  • 3 tbsp curry paste
  • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
  • 200 gr frozen spinach
  • 1 tin coconut milk
  • rice
  • yoghurt (optional)

Steps

  • Tip the beans, curry paste, chopped tomatoes, spinach and coconut milk into a medium sized pan over a medium heat. Stir everything together well.
  • Bring to a gentle simmer then reduce heat and let cook away for 10 minutes, until the spinach is fully defrosted and the sauce is slightly thickened.
  • Layer your ingredients in a medium sized ovenproof container.Taste and season with salt and pepper if needed (it will vary depending on the curry paste used). Serve with rice and yoghurt.
